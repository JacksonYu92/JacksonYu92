How I start

The pandemic lockdown has created too much free time for me, so I decided to get into web development in early 2020. ZERO experience in programming prior. I continued coding little by little, getting stuck a lot and even thinking that maybe coding wasn't for someone like me. I made my first ever HTML page in April. Then, I learned about CSS and JavaScript from online courses on Udemy.

Challenge

When life back to normal, I have to work for two jobs at the same time. Work + study have been challenging me. So I made a new habit of waking up before 5 am. Do 2 ~ 3 hours of study before work and 1 ~ 2 hours of study at night for Monday to Friday. I tried to learn as much as I can over every weekend. I am constantly pushing myself out of my comfort zone to learn more and hopefully make my dream come true one day in the future. I mainly learned from Udemy, freeCodeCamp, and The Odin Project. As a full-stack developer, I enjoy learning new stuff daily and building amazing things.
